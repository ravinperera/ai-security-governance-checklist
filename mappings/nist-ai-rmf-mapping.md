# NIST AI RMF Mapping

This lightweight mapping aligns practical AI governance activities with the NIST AI Risk Management Framework functions: Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage.

## Govern

Controls that establish accountability and policy.

- Assign AI governance owner.
- Maintain approved AI tools register.
- Define enterprise AI usage policy.
- Define vendor approval process.
- Define exception process.
- Train employees on acceptable AI use.

Related files:

- `checklists/ai-governance-checklist.md`
- `checklists/enterprise-ai-usage-policy.md`
- `templates/approved-ai-tools-register.csv`

## Map

Controls that identify context, purpose, users, data, and impact.

- Maintain AI system inventory.
- Record business purpose and owner.
- Classify data used by the AI system.
- Identify affected users and stakeholders.
- Identify integrations, tools, vendors, and model providers.

Related files:

- `templates/ai-system-inventory.csv`
- `checklists/ai-vendor-assessment.md`

## Measure

Controls that assess AI risks and performance.

- Perform AI risk assessment.
- Test prompt injection and unsafe outputs.
- Review privacy, security, legal, and operational risks.
- Track known limitations and failure modes.
- Review model output quality for critical use cases.

Related files:

- `templates/ai-risk-register.csv`
- `checklists/llm-application-security-checklist.md`

## Manage

Controls that mitigate, monitor, and improve AI risk management.

- Assign risk treatment owners.
- Define mitigations and due dates.
- Monitor AI incidents and misuse.
- Review approved tools periodically.
- Reassess vendors and high-risk systems.
- Update policies after incidents or major AI changes.

Related files:

- `templates/ai-risk-register.csv`
- `checklists/shadow-ai-risk-checklist.md`
