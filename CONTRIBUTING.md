# Contributing

Contributions are welcome.

This project is intended to be practical, clear, and usable by real engineering, security, compliance, and governance teams.

## Good Contributions

- New checklist items with clear wording
- Practical templates
- Framework mappings
- Vendor assessment questions
- AI risk examples
- Improvements for DevOps, SaaS, ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and engineering teams

## Style

- Keep wording simple.
- Avoid legal jargon where plain language works.
- Prefer actionable checklist items.
- Do not include vendor marketing.
- Do not include confidential company material.

## Pull Request Checklist

- [ ] The change is practical and reusable.
- [ ] No confidential data is included.
- [ ] No legal advice is presented as final legal guidance.
- [ ] Markdown tables render correctly.
- [ ] Templates remain easy to copy and use.
