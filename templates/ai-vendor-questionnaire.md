# AI Vendor Questionnaire

Use this questionnaire during vendor security and privacy review.

## Vendor Details

1. What is the legal name of the vendor?
2. What product or AI feature is being assessed?
3. What AI model provider is used?
4. Where is the service hosted?
5. Which regions process or store customer data?

## Data Usage

1. What customer data is processed by the AI system?
2. Is customer data used to train or improve models?
3. Can training on customer data be disabled?
4. What prompt and output data is stored?
5. What is the retention period for prompts and outputs?
6. How can data be deleted?

## Security Controls

1. Do you support SSO/SAML/OIDC?
2. Do you support MFA?
3. Do you support role-based access control?
4. Are admin actions logged?
5. Are user prompts and outputs logged?
6. Is data encrypted in transit and at rest?
7. Do you perform vulnerability scanning and penetration testing?
8. Do you have SOC 2, ISO 27001, or equivalent assurance reports?

## AI-Specific Risks

1. How do you mitigate prompt injection?
2. How do you prevent sensitive information disclosure?
3. How do you handle insecure or harmful outputs?
4. How are model updates tested before release?
5. Can customers control model versions?
6. Are plugins, tools, or agents available?
7. How are tool permissions controlled?

## Privacy And Compliance

1. Do you provide a Data Processing Agreement?
2. Which subprocessors are used?
3. How are international data transfers handled?
4. How do you support data subject requests?
5. How do you notify customers about breaches?

## Exit

1. How can customer data be exported?
2. How can customer data be deleted?
3. What happens to prompts, outputs, embeddings, and logs after termination?
